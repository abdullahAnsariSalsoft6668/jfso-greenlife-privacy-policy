Privacy & Refund Policy

Privacy Policy

JFSO GreenLife respects your privacy. We only collect basic details like your name, contact information, and order or donation history to process purchases and provide customer support.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Your data is only used to complete your orders and donations securely.

Donations

Part of the proceeds from purchases and donations support local farmers and charitable causes in Jamaica. Donations are used responsibly to help communities in need.

Refund Policy

You may request a refund or exchange for physical products within 14 days of delivery, if the item is unused and in original condition. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Donations and digital services (such as memorials) are non-refundable.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Cash on Delivery is available for eligible orders. Please have the exact amount ready, as delivery staff may not carry change.
